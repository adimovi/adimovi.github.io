How would I know if my venue is suitable?

Is it in a reasonably central city location,or significant suburb or a tourist resort?
Does it have a minimum of 200 square meters/2,000 square feet?
Is it a reasonably open plan design?
Does it have or the potential to have a smoking area, which is not on the street?
If the answer to the above questions is yes, you are off to a good start.

What will it cost to redesign my venue to include coworking activities during the day?

That will depend on each venue, and the costs per location, but in general it will be anywhere between 50k and 100k Euro equivalent.
