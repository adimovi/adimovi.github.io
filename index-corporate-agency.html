QR Mode

+

Incorporate QR Mode Interaction into your strategy to captivate your audience with unmatched cost efficiency.

Starting at just $0.05, this feature promises to engage your customers like never before. If the bid for your ad's price exceeds $0.05, the QR interaction cost aligns with your ad price. Importantly, the QR interaction price is charged only if the user decides to interact with it.

This ensures your investment is directly tied to actual engagement, maximizing your advertising spend's return. QR Mode transforms each ad into a potential interactive journey, offering viewers the chance to explore coupons, appointments, URLs, or app downloads with a simple scan. It shifts viewers from passive observation to active engagement, providing accurate engagement metrics to optimize campaign performance.

Step into the world of QR Mode and transform your advertising into immersive experiences that attract and engage your audience, ensuring you pay only for genuine interactions.

Store Mode

+

Step into the future with STORE Mode Interaction, designed to elevate customer engagement to unprecedented levels with unmatched efficiency.

Here's how it works: if your ad spend is $0.01 and you opt for STORE Interaction, an activation cost of $0.05 is applied, making the total $0.06 if a user activates the Store menu. Additionally, a 5% fee is charged on the total order value, should the customer proceed to place an order (like ordering a pizza, for instance).

On the other hand, if your ad spend is higher, say $0.08, and you choose to activate STORE Interaction, the Store menu interaction cost adjusts to match your higher ad spend, totaling $0.08 for the ad itself and another $0.08 for the Store interaction activation if a user engages with it. Plus, the 5% fee on the total order value still applies for completed purchases.
