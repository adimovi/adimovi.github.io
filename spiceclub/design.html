Spice Clubs Designs

Spice Clubs have been designed very carefully by Barry Murphy from Ireland so that they create a mix of rustic and yet modern contemporary class, which transcends all global cultures.

The rustic part of the story is designed to create an atmosphere which encourages drink sales, and the contemporary elegant class touches are designed to attract the right type of members.

We believe that we have come up with a good subtle balance to create a 300 square meters (3,000 square feet) space of elegance, where up to 170 members can socialise freely in a warm and friendly safe environment.

The concept of turning on or off the small light on each table so that members can tell who is happy to be approached or accept company or not, makes it very easy and comfortable for all members to know what the situation is.

Creating different size spaces so that all members can feel comfortable regardless of the number at any table was a very important element of the design process, while at the same time designing the club to ensure that it is extremely easy for solo members to meet and mix with others effortlessly.

The message of Spice Clubs is very simple for those interested in any type of dating relationship/arrangement.

Forget about messing around wasting time online at home on your own, and enjoy your time meeting real interesting people in person.
