If you were able to work from anywhere remotely, where would you live?

The concept of earning a western standard salary and living in a more affordable and often much better location, in many cases can increase your spending power by a factor of 3.

This difference can be used to create an amazing quality lifestyle that you could never afford at home on the same salary, or to save a lot of money over a few years, while still living the lifestyle you were used to in your home country.

If you like the outdoor life, would you select to live in a 5 star golf and spa course village, which is also adjacent to a beautiful ski area in Bulgaria?

Your monthly rent for a nice 1 bedroom apartment with stunning views is 500 Euro, and your living expenses for the month can be as little as 700 Euro, if you want them to be.
