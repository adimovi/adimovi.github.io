The first franchise partner in any designated area will always get first preference on subsequent club licence agreements as demand expands in their area.

What size or capacity is each Spice Club?

Each Spice club requires approximately 300 square meters(3,000 square feet) of space, and has capacity for 75 tables, or upto 150 patrons.

What does it cost to open a Spice Club outside of property lease expenses?

The refit cost of the premises is usually in the region of 150k Euro, outside of local labor expenses, and we provide all of the designs, as well as the materials within that cost.

How Many staff is required to operate a Spice Club?

Six staff on site each evening.

What is the expected net annual income for each Spice Club?

We will provide calculated expected financial details to interested parties, but as a general rule of thumb, we would hope that each club would generate in the region of 250k to 350k euro net per annum for franchise partners, depending on location.

What other support does Birchley Enterprises Ireland provide Franchise Partners?

We provide the booking app for all locations worldwide and we pay our franchise partners their share of the booking fees weekly.

We take responsibility for the global marketing strategy and execution, but we do require some small assistance from local franchise partners on an ongoing basis.

Who do we accept as franchise partners?

We look for three ingredients in any potential franchise partner. The first is the financial capacity, the second is a reliable past business record, and lastly to make sure they have the right communication, social skills for the hospitality industry.

How does one go about applying to be a Spice Club franchise partner for their location?

Step 1 is to fill in the application form below.

Step 2 is to get on a zoom call with Barry Murphy the founder of Spice Clubs.

Step 3 is that if all are happy, we will provide a lot of information on margins, designs, and operations.

Step 4 will be to sign preliminary franchise partner contracts, once an approved venue has been selected and secured.

Step 5 will be to sign the full contract and pay 30% of the refurbishment materials cost, with the remaining 70% being paid upon proof of shipping documents for all materials.

Step 6 will be to refit the club and get it open, up and running.

What materials are in the refurbishment package?

We give all our partners the benefit of buying in bulk to keep the refit expenses to a minimum, and also to make sure that all clubs worldwide have the same familiar feel for our customers.

  • Timber flooring
  • Tables and seating
  • Bathroom suites
  • Bar counter and backdrop
  • Display Fridges
  • POS till system
  • Camera system
  • Access to your clubs booking dashboard
  • Large TV screens, and sound system, and we will also provide the content to be displayed which attracts the type of customer we want.
  • All logos and signage.
  • All lighting fixtures and fittings.

Our engineers/architects will design and calculate the quantity of materials required, once we have drawings of the approved premises.

From start to finish, it should take 12 weeks to have any club ready for opening. 6 weeks preparation work, and a maximum of another 6 weeks after the refurbishment materials arrive.
