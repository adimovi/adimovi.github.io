Open Your Own Spice Club

Why would you want to open your own Spice Club in your location?

The first reason is that itâ€™s a very lucrative business because it is not reliant on weekend trade like most clubs. The reason for this is that a lot of our members are often travelling for business or vacation, which can make weekdays even busier than weekends in some cases.

The second reason is that we do not need expensive high street property venues, because we do not need or want passing trade.

The first venue partner in any designated area will always get first preference on subsequent club licence agreements as demand expands in their area.

What size or capacity is each Spice Club?

Each Spice club requires approximately 300 square meters(3,000 square feet) of space, and has capacity for 75 tables, or up to 150 patrons.

What does it cost to open a Spice Club outside of property lease expenses?

That completely depends on the current state of the venue, but here are a few pointers:

1. Our Tables and seating have to be used, which are approximately 130 euro per piece. 30k per club approx.

2. Bathrooms need to be of a high standard.

3. Timber flooring is required.

4. We have a team of architects who will work with each venue partner to keep costs down and standards up.

How Many staff is required to operate a Spice Club?

Six staff on site each evening.

What is the expected net annual income for each Spice Club?

We will provide calculated expected financial details to interested parties, but as a general rule of thumb, we would hope that each club would generate in the region of 250k to 350k euro net per annum for venue partners, depending on location.

What other support does Birchley Enterprises Ireland provide Venue Partners?

1. Refit design assistance to keep costs low and standards high.

2. Access to our online booking system for members.

3. Continuous benefit from our global marketing strategies.

4. Continuous operating support from our HQ team.

5. The use of our Trade Name and Signage, subject to conditions as per partner legal contract.

Who do we accept as franchise partners?

We look for three ingredients in any potential franchise partner. The first is the financial capacity, the second is a reliable past business record, and lastly to make sure they have the right communication, social skills for the hospitality industry.

How does one go about applying to be a Spice Club franchise partner for their location?

Step 1 is to fill in the application form below.

Step 2 is to get on a zoom call with Barry Murphy the founder of Spice Clubs.

Step 3 is that if all are happy, we will provide a lot of information on margins, designs, and operations.

Step 4 will be to sign franchise partner contracts, once an approved venue has been selected and secured.

Step 5 will be to prepare the venue for opening with our design assistance.
